Mom's Cholent

A traditional slow-cooked Jewish stew that's perfect for special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 cup beans
  • 1 cup meat
  • 3 onions
  • 4 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 cup of barley
  • 5 large potatoes, peeled and cut into thirds
  • boiling water to cover
  • 2 packages of dry onion and mushroom soup mix

Steps

  1. In a large oven safe pot or roasting pan, saute onions in oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the rice, beans, and meat to the pot.
  3. Stir in the barley and potatoes.
  4. Pour in the boiling water and soup mix.
  5. Cover and slow cook for eight hours until the stew is tender.
