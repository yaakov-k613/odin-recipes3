Mom's Turkey Recipe

A traditional holiday dish that's perfect for family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole turkey (12-14 lbs)
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. Rinse the turkey and pat it dry.
  3. Season the turkey inside and out with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the turkey in a roasting pan and brush with melted butter.
  5. Bake for 3-4 hours or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
